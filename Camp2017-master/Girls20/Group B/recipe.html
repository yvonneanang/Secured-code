HOW TO PREPARE RICE

Rice is easy to cook. It is delicious to eat.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • water
  • salt

METHOD

  1. Fetch a cup of rice into a saucepan.
  2. Fetch one and half cups of water and pour in the same saucepan.
  3. Take one and half teaspoons of salt and add to the mixture in the saucepan to taste.
  4. Place the saucepan on the gas cooker.
  5. Light up the gas cooker and lower the flame.
  6. Cover the saucepan and leave the rice to boil for ten minutes
  7. Check at regular intervals to see whether rice is fully cooked

Rice is soft when properly cooked. It can be eaten with a variety of soups and stews.

DISH ON A PLATE AND ENJOY YOUR MEAL!!!
